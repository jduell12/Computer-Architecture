# Number Bases

- Base 2 - binary
- Base 8 - octal
- Base 10 - decimal
- Base 16 - hexadecimal ("hex")
- Base 64 - "base 64"

- Base 10 - numbering system we learn in school. Not convenient for computers. 
    - Digits 0-9
    - Shows number based on the power of 10
        ex. 12 --> one 10 and two 1s ... 1 * 10 + 2 * 1 = 12
        - 1s place = 10^0
        - 10s place = 10^1
        - 100s = 10^2
        - 1000s = 10^3
